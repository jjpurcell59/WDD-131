Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to inform, engage, and entertain energy drink consumers by offering detailed reviews, public‑based ratings, historical context, and interactive features. Users can explore drinks based on popularity, rating, availability, and more.

Audience

The site targets energy drink consumers, students, athletes, and curious individuals who want to learn more about their favorite beverages, discover new ones, or reminisce about discontinued classics.

Dynamic elements

Dynamic elements include: interactive star‑based ratings, sortable and filterable drink cards, modal pop‑ups for additional details, and responsiveness to user interactions such as clicks and selections. The site uses modules, functions, arrays, and DOM manipulation to achieve interactivity.
